Understanding Basis Sets

Different basis sets provide different levels of accuracy and computational cost:

  • STO-3G: Minimal basis, fast computations, lower accuracy
  • 6-31G: Split-valence basis, good balance of accuracy and speed
  • cc-pVDZ: Correlation-consistent basis, higher accuracy for correlated methods
